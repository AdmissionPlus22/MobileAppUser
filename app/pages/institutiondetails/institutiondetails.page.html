About Us:

Delhi Public School Megacity is an academic institution that follows a progressive educational system. The school follows a curriculum that truly believes in developing life skills in students and making them global citizens. We are affiliated with CISCE Board (ICSE and ISC).

The school promotes a holistic learning system that caters to all-around development of its students and provides a challenging environment for students to reach their optimum potential. Our school was established on 25th April 2004 and is situated in the city of Kolkata, West Bengal, India. It is a co-educational school with over 6050 students equipped with modern technology. We follow the latest teaching pedagogy and have received numerous awards in the field of academic excellence, sports, and various other curricular activities.

 

SCHOOL PROFILE

DPS MEGACITY is an ISO accredited and ISA recognized educational institution(for the second time), which has been recently awarded the much coveted The Telegraph School of the Year Award. In recent times it has also been embellished with honors like The Telegraph award for the ‘School with Best Co- Curricular Practices’ the ‘Sankalp Awards for Best Co-Curricular Practices’ and ‘The Gurukul Award for the Best Maintained School’. The Times of India had rated the school as the best in Eastern India among the upcoming schools. The school also has received the prestigious ‘Daan Utsav Samman’ for its overall contribution to society. These are some of the many awards that the school has been adorned in recent years. The school, in only seventeen years of its evolutionary journey, has established itself as a premiere educational institution for its sense of genuine concern and profound understanding of children and their world, state-of-the-art infrastructure which includes laboratories, football field, basketball, tennis and volleyball Courts, swimming Pool and a shooting range, equal importance to curricular and co-curricular activities, individual attention, global interaction, the earnestness to create holistic individuals for tomorrow’s society, providing equal opportunities to the underprivileged, sincere commitment to social service, a dynamic system of growth, and creation of individual leaders. Today, it is one of the fastest growing educational institutions in the country, which has been able to strike an exquisite balance between the curricular and co-curricular.

In the last fourteen years, our results at the ICSE and ISC Examinations have been consistently brilliant, featuring toppers at the State and National levels. Our performance at the various Olympiads and pan India competitive examinations has been quite brilliant.

DPS MEGACITY is a school that nurtures the present on the pedestal of the future. A state-of-the-art infrastructure, sports facilities that adhere to national standards, air conditioning in all classrooms, solar panels to supplement the electricity supply, adherence to the innovations and modernization in the field of education, maintaining the highest performance standards nationally in the board examinations, and a rare and exquisite balance between the curricular and co-curricular make the school a true harbinger of the most promising and dynamic educational future.

Well-equipped laboratories, well-resourced libraries, a most prolific chain of subject application based events, participation in the most coveted co-curricular competitions in the country and curving a lasting niche there, the enrollment of students in the best of the universities after passing out from DPS MEGACITY, the faculty being honored and felicitated on a regular basis, give the school a unique identity, one of time transcending competence and preparedness for all the unpredictability of tomorrow.
